HOW TO ADD YOUR PHOTOS
======================

1. Download your photos from Instagram or your phone.
2. Rename them to match the following pattern:
   - photo-1.jpg
   - photo-2.jpg
   - photo-3.jpg
   - photo-4.jpg
   - photo-5.jpg
   - photo-6.jpg
3. Drag and drop them into this "photos" folder, replacing any existing files.
4. Refresh your website to see them!
